## Slide 2: The Problem
**"The Lateral Entry Gap"**
*   **Academic Disparity:** Missing 1st-year foundations (Math, Coding).
*   **Social Isolation:** Joining pre-formed peer groups is hard.
*   **Career Confusion:** "Am I too late for internships?"
*   **Result:** Lower confidence, higher dropout risk, delayed placements.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution
**"Bridge2Success Ecosystem"**
A dedicated web platform designed to **Level the Playing Field**.
*   **Connect:** Find peers and mentors instantly.
*   **Catch Up:** Personalized academic bridging resources.
*   **Conquer:** Clear career roadmaps and opportunities.

---

## Slide 4: Key Modules
1.  **Academic Bridge:**
    *   Stream-specific onboarding (CSE vs. Mech).
    *   Video lessons & Notes for gap subjects.
2.  **Social Hub:**
    *   Join Study Groups (e.g., "GATE 2026").
    *   Connect with Lateral Entry Alumni Mentors.
3.  **Career Launchpad:**
    *   Internship aggregator (filtered for lateral timelines).
    *   Visual Skill Roadmaps.
